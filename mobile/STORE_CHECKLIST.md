# Store Listing Checklist

Fill these in once before submitting to either store.

## Identity
- **App name:** Business Escalate
- **Subtitle / short description:** "Level up your life — fitness, food & income."
- **Bundle / package id:** `com.businessescalate.app`
- **Category:** Health & Fitness (or Productivity)
- **Primary color / theme:** `#0F1117`

## Description (draft)
> Business Escalate is your daily life-progress coach. Track the areas that
> matter — workouts, nutrition (with a calorie + macro calculator and a food
> counter), networking, income, reading, water and weight — in a 30-second daily
> check-in. An AI coach reads your real data and tells you exactly what to do
> next. Make the app your own: rename, add, or switch off any of the areas you
> track. Your data stays private to your account.

## Keywords (Apple) / tags
habit tracker, fitness, nutrition, macros, calorie counter, weight, productivity,
goals, AI coach, journal

## Required assets
- **App icon:** generated by `npm run assets` from `assets/logo.svg` (1024×1024).
- **Screenshots:** capture from a running device/emulator.
  - iPhone 6.7" (1290×2796) and 6.5"; iPad if you support it.
  - Android phone (min 2), feature graphic 1024×500 for Play.
- **Splash screen:** generated by `npm run assets`.

## Privacy & compliance (both stores require this)
- **Privacy policy URL** — mandatory. It must state what you collect
  (account username, the tracking data they enter, optional health/nutrition
  info) and that it's stored on your server. Host it as a page (a `/privacy`
  route on the cloud app works).
- **Data Safety form (Play) / App Privacy (Apple):** declare:
  - Account info (username) — for app functionality.
  - Health & fitness data (workouts, weight, nutrition) — stored, not shared, not sold.
  - No third-party ad tracking.
- **Account deletion (Apple requirement):** apps with accounts must let users
  delete their account in-app. → add a "Delete my account" action (calls a
  `DELETE /api/account` endpoint — I can add this when you're ready).
- **AI disclosure:** note that coaching/insights are AI-generated estimates, not
  professional medical/financial advice (especially for the nutrition features).

## Pre-submit smoke test
- Sign up, log a day, see the dashboard + AI insight.
- Log out / log back in (token persists).
- Forgot-password flow works.
- Export a backup; import it.
- Looks right on a real phone (responsive).

## Nice-to-have before launch (reduces Apple rejection risk)
- Native push notifications (`@capacitor/push-notifications`) for the daily reminder.
- "Delete account" in Settings.
