Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 June 2026

Escalate ("we", "us", the "app") is a personal life-tracking app that helps you log and improve your fitness, nutrition, finances, networking, reading and habits, with an optional AI coach. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the choices you have. We built Escalate to be private by default: we do not sell your data and we do not use advertising trackers.

1. Information we collect

  • Account details — your username, email address and (optionally) phone number, used to create and secure your account. Your password is stored only as a salted hash; we never see it in plain text. If you set a security question, we store it to let you reset your password.
  • The data you log — whatever you choose to track: workouts, food and calories, weight and body details (height, age, sex used to calculate your nutrition targets), income and spending, contacts you add, reading, business ideas, vocabulary, notes, checklists and water. This is your personal data and is tied to your account.
  • Notification data — if you enable reminders/push, we store a push subscription so we can deliver them. You can turn this off at any time.
  • Technical data — a sign-in token stored in your browser to keep you logged in, and basic server logs needed to run and secure the service.
  • Payment data — if you subscribe, payment is handled by our payment processor (Stripe). We never receive or store your card number.

2. How we use your information

  • To run the app: save your logs, show your stats, streaks, charts and goal progress.
  • To generate AI coaching and insights, when you use those features (see §3).
  • To send the reminders and notifications you choose to enable.
  • To process a subscription, if you start one.
  • To keep accounts secure and prevent abuse.

We do not use your personal data for advertising, and we do not sell or rent it to anyone.

3. The AI Coach and third parties we rely on

Some features send your data to trusted providers purely to deliver the service:

  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — when you use the AI coach, food estimates or insights, the relevant tracked data and profile are sent to Anthropic's API to generate a response. Direct contact details (email and phone) are removed before anything is sent. This data is processed to answer your request and is not used to train AI models.
  • Stripe — payment processing for subscriptions.
  • Render & Neon — secure hosting and database where your account data is stored.
  • Browser / OS push services — used only to deliver notifications you enable.

4. Community posts

If you choose to share something to the in-app Community, that post is visible to other users of the app. Please don't post anything private or sensitive there. You can delete your own posts, and we may remove content that is flagged or breaks our rules.

5. Your choices and rights

  • Access & export — you can export a copy of your data at any time from Settings → Backup.
  • Deletion — you can ask us to delete your account and data by emailing us at the address below; we'll action it promptly.
  • Notifications — turn reminders and nudges on or off in Settings / Checklist.
  • Profile — edit or remove the details you've added at any time.

6. Data security & retention

Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS), passwords are hashed, and access requires a signed token. We keep your data for as long as your account is active, or until you ask us to delete it. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect your information.

7. Children

Escalate is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. International users

Your data may be processed on servers located in other countries. By using Escalate you consent to this transfer and processing.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll change the "last updated" date above, and significant changes will be highlighted in the app.
