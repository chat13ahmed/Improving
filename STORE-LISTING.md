## Full description (Google Play: max 4000 characters)

**One app for your whole life — and an AI coach that sees how it all connects.**

Most trackers do one thing. Escalate brings your gym, nutrition, money, reading, networking and habits into one place, then shows you how they pull on each other — so a strong week in one area lifts the rest.

Every day you log takes you one step further up the climb. Watch your streaks grow, your goals fill in, and your progress turn into something you'll actually want to share.

**WHAT YOU CAN TRACK**
• 🏋️ Workouts, training split and a gym plan for your goal
• 🥗 Calories and macros, with a meal-by-meal "eat now" guide
• ⚖️ Weight over time — see your body shape change as you do
• 💰 Income, spending and savings goals
• 🤝 People you meet and connections you make
• 📚 Reading, with a 220-book library and a vocabulary builder
• 💡 Business ideas, notes, water and a daily checklist

**WHAT MAKES IT DIFFERENT**
• 🧗 The climb — every log moves you up the mountain toward your goal
• 🤖 An AI coach that reads your whole picture and tells you your next move
• 🔗 "Connections" that reveal how your habits affect each other
• 🎯 A clean dashboard that shows how much of your weekly goals you've hit
• 🔥 Streaks, milestones and a one-tap weekly recap to share your progress
• 🔔 Gentle reminders so you never break your chain

**PRIVATE BY DESIGN**
Your data is yours. No ads, no trackers, and we never sell your information. Export or delete it anytime.

Start your climb today — track one life, and watch every part of it rise together.
